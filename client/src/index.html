Obtuse Octo Adventure

(because that is what you get when you let GitHub name your project)

A simple blackjack implementation

Rule specifics:

  • Dealer always stays on 17 or higher
  • Dealer always hits on 16 or higher
  • Player wins if they beat the dealer (without going bust)
  • Dealer wins if no players win
  • Dealer automatically wins on 21
  • Aces are worth exactly 11
